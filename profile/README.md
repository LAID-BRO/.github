# LAID

**Legacy Alumni in Data Intelligence**

---

## Our Vision

LAID is an emerging alumni community born from a shared foundation in data intelligence and analytics. We are a network of graduates who continue to push boundaries, share knowledge, and support each other's growth in the field of AI and data science.

Our mission is to:
- **Connect** alumni across different stages of their careers
- **Showcase** innovative work and achievements of our community members
- **Foster** collaboration and knowledge sharing
- **Build** a supportive network that extends beyond graduation

## What We're Building

As a young community, we're in the early stages of building our platform. We envision:

- A space to share research, projects, and insights
- Resources and learning materials for continuous growth
- Opportunities for collaboration and mentorship
- A network that celebrates both individual achievements and collective progress

## Join Us

We welcome all alumni who share our passion for data intelligence and innovation. Whether you're just starting out or have years of experience, there's a place for you here.

*This community is growing. Stay tuned for updates as we build our platform together.*
